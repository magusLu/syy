LIFE SCIENCE & HEALTH

The Division of Life and Health Sciences consists of 5 research areas, including Biology, Chemical Biology, Marine Biology, Biomedical Engineering, and Optical Imaging and Sensing. Totally, over 10 directions of research are involved. They are molecular biology, cell biology, plant molecular biology, cancer biology, gene therapy, pharmaceutics and pharmacology, Metabonomics ,Speech Processing and Digital Medical Equipment R & D.　Projects in study by the division in 2010 include Chinese National Natural Science Foundation, 863 Project and Drug Mega Discovery Project, Natural Science Foundation of Guangdong Province, Shenzhen-Hong Kong Innovation Circle Fund, Shenzhen Science and Technology Bureau Foundation. The projects are funded by NSFC, the Ministry of Science and Technology, the Ministry of Education, Guangdong provincial and Shenzhen municipal governments. The Division of Life and Health Sciences have many patent applications and SCI publications each year.
