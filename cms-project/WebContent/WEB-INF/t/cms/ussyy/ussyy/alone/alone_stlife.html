STUDENT LIFE

Tsinghua University attracts the brightest students from all over the country. The Graduate School at Shenzhen shares the fame, the strength and the resources of the main campus, and contributes to Tsinghua's endeavour to be a world-class university. On campus, field study,bilingual teaching, English activities, creative courses, and humanities lectures represent the innovative teaching style of the Graduate School. Student organisations are flourishing! Activities like career planning lectures, singing competitions, fun and games, debates, and psychodrama all enrich the campus life.

Fashion events

After a hard day's study, going out and having fun is what student life is all about. On top of which, there are lots of student activities like theatrical performances to get involved in. It's the perfect way to make friends and add a bit of sparkle to your CV. The traditional learning style of Tsinghua University, 'Rigorous, Diligent,Realistic and Creative' is practised on this dynamic campus.Tsinghua's motto, 'Self-discipline and Social Commitment' is ingrained in the hearts of all the students. Faculty, staff and students are all dedicated to the mission of building Tsinghua University into a worldclass university. They enjoy the glory of Tsinghua and the dream of a prosperous future.
