INTERNATIONAL

The Graduate School at Shenzhen, Tsinghua University strives to serve the development of the regional and national economies and contribute to Tsinghua's endeavor to become a world-class university. The School aims to achieve academic preeminence in research and teaching by transplanting key disciplines from the main campus to Shenzhen, developing new disciplines with local characteristics and encouraging interdisciplinary collaborations. Its seven divisions offer (a number) Masters and doctoral programs.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

  • CHEMICAL BIOLOGY
  • BIOLOGICAL ENGINEERING
  • OPTICAL DETECTION AND IMAGING

RESEARCH INITIATIVES

  • INSTITUTES FOR HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT
    The Institute for Hospital Management is a university-level interdisciplinary research institute founded in 2012 dedicated to becoming a leader in the field of hospital management research, training hospital management professionals, and providing theoretical and technical support for the development of China's healthcare industry.
